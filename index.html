    Features

    Everything you need to capture your travels

    GPS Journey Recording

    Automatically track your route as you drive. Our battery-efficient GPS recording captures every twist and turn of your adventure.

    Photo Memories

    Post photos and updates from the road. Link them to your recorded journeys, highlight hidden gems, and inspire fellow friends.

    Travel Diary

    Write diary entries at each stop. Document your experiences, favourite campsites, and hidden gems you discover along the way.

    Connect & Share

    Follow other explorers, share your voyages, and get inspired by adventures from the motorhome community across the UK and Europe.

    Trip Summaries

    See your total miles traveled, countries visited, and days on the road. Track your motorhome mileage and look back on years of adventure.

    Discover Stops

    Access thousands of motorhome-friendly stops across Europe. Find campsites, aires, and wild camping spots recommended by the community.

    Voyages

    Group multiple journeys into a voyage — like “French Alps Summer 2025” — with a timeline, map highlights, and trip stats.

    Vehicle tracking

    Track mileage, service history, and maintenance reminders — built for motorhomes and RVs.

    Cost tracking

    Log campsite costs, fuel, tolls, electricity, and more — so you can understand what trips really cost.

    Message other users

    Send messages to fellow travellers, ask questions, and share tips — without leaving the app.

    Explore countries & regions

    A “Nomad List for motorhomers” — compare countries and regions by cost, facilities, roads, wild camping ease, and more.
