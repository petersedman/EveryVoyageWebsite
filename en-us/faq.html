FAQ

    Common questions

    Quick answers to things we get asked often about EveryVoyage.

    Is EveryVoyage really free?

    Yes! The core app is completely free. Founding Members get free access for life, including any premium features we add later.

    Which devices are supported?

    EveryVoyage is available on iOS and Android. Download from the App Store or Google Play.

    Does it work offline?

    Yes, GPS recording works without an internet connection. Your journeys sync when you're back online.

    How do I become a Founding Member?

    Just download the app and create an account while spots are available. It's that simple!

    How does GPS recording work?

    Tap "Record" when you start your journey. EveryVoyage runs in the background, tracking your route with battery-efficient GPS. Stop recording when you arrive.

    Can I share my journeys?

    Absolutely! You can share your voyages with friends, family, or the EveryVoyage community. Choose who sees your adventures.

    What data do you collect?

    We only collect what's needed to make the app work. Your location data stays on your device until you choose to sync. See our Privacy Policy for details.

    How do I contact support?

    Email us at support@everyvoyage.com. We typically respond within 24 hours and love hearing from fellow travelers.

    Still have questions?

    We're here to help. Get in touch and we'll get back to you as soon as we can.
