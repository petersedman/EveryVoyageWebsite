This Privacy Policy explains how SIMPLR LABS LTD (“we”, “us”, “our”) collects and uses personal data when you use the EveryVoyage mobile application and related website features, including the Explore section at everyvoyage.com (together, the “Service”).

1. Personal data we collect

  • Account data: email address, username, and profile details.

We use your personal data to:

Our legal bases include performance of a contract, consent (where required), legitimate interests, and legal obligation, as applicable.

Some information, such as public profiles, trips, locations, photos, reviews, and comments, may be visible to other users and the public depending on how you use the Service.

4. Analytics and diagnostics

We use third-party tools to help operate and improve the Service, including:

  • Firebase for analytics and notifications.
  • Sentry for crash reporting and diagnostics.

5. Data sharing

We may share personal data with trusted service providers who process data on our behalf strictly as necessary to operate the Service, or where required by law. We do not sell personal data.

6. International transfers

As we operate globally, some service providers may process data outside the United Kingdom. Where required, appropriate safeguards are applied in accordance with UK data protection law.

7. Data retention

We retain personal data only for as long as necessary for service provision, legal compliance, security, and abuse prevention.

8. Your rights

You may have rights to access, correct, delete, restrict, object to processing, or request portability of your personal data, and to withdraw consent where applicable.

To exercise your rights, contact us at contact@everyvoyage.com. UK users may also complain to the Information Commissioner’s Office (ICO).

9. Account deletion

You may delete your account at any time via:

You → Settings → Delete account

You may also request deletion by emailing contact@everyvoyage.com from your registered email address.

10. Security

We use appropriate technical and organisational measures to protect personal data. No system is completely secure.

11. Children

The Service is not intended for children under the age of 13, or a higher minimum age where required by local law.

12. Updates to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Material changes will be communicated through the Service.
